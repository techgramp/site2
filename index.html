About Gerri: It was a leap of faith to leave the corporate world and begin pet grooming. It was my passion and I wanted to pursue it. I never looked back after receiving my Groomers Certification. The service industry was a perfect fit for me. I started grooming in Alpharetta, GA and was there for almost 20 years. From puppies to senior dogs, and comfort grooms for the infirmed pets. I always enjoyed going to work. I understand the client, groomer, pet relationship. You want someone you can trust. Someone compassionate, someone in for the long term, someone with heart. Hey Dood Pet Grooming is proud to provide these services for Camden County.
